Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Apex Fraction-Al ("we", "our", "us") operates as a professional IT Consultancy. We are fully committed to guarding the personal privacy of clients, prospects, and visitors to this website in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Information We Collect

We process personal data provided strictly through direct communications, including telephone conversations, formal business network avenues, or electronic mail. This data includes:

  • Individual Full Names and Corporate/Business Titles.
  • Contact infrastructure (Email Addresses, Mobile Numbers).
  • Strategic notes, transactional criteria, and contextual scope details provided regarding potential consulting engagements.

2. How Data is Utilized

Your data is systematically handled strictly for the following lawful criteria:

  • To evaluate contract parameters or deploy agreed-upon services (Performance of Contract).
  • To respond to outbound business inquiries or consultation workflows (Legitimate Interests).
  • To fulfill standard regulatory accounting or statutory reporting requirements (Legal Obligation).

3. Data Sharing and Retention

We do not lease, trade, or distribute your private tracking or contact data to third parties. Data is kept safely tucked behind enterprise security measures and only maintained as long as required to adequately manage business relationships or legal accounting timelines.

4. Your Rights Under GDPR

You have full legal rights regarding the storage of your information. You may request copies of personal data held, demand corrections to structural errors, or request immediate full deletion from active databases.
